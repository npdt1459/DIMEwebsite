Revenue Model

  • Freemium: Basic navigation, traffic updates, and transport tracking fee.
  • Subscription: Premium features including ad-free use, priority support, and AI suggestions.
  • Advertisements: Local businesses can advertise in the app (basic or premium listings).
  • Transit Commissions: 1% commission on Leap Card top-ups.

Growth Strategy

  • Launch in Ireland with targeted digital and influencer marketing.
  • Expand to wider European market within 3–5 years.
  • Use freemium model with early adopter discounts to gain traction.

Projected Revenue (1st Year)

For the first three months, our main revenue will be by angel investors and crowdfunding. By the end of these three months, we aim to have 500 users, with a 10% conversion rate. This gets us started with earning at least €FILL a month in membership fees.

Assume 500 users open the app 5 times a day and see 2 interstitial (fullscreen) ads each time: We can expect €1,050 a month in ad revenue. Once we reach 2,000 users, we can expect €4,200 a month.

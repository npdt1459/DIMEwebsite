User Personas

Katie

Age: 19

Occupation: College Student

Location: Dublin, Ireland

Katie is a busy student at DCU, and she gets the bus to college every day and finds it frustrating switching between different apps to top-up her Leap Card and check bus times. She often studies late in the library and has to travel home alone at night and worries about her safety.

How NavRide Helps: Katie can top up her Leap Card and check her bus schedules in just one app. Katie can also use NavRide's crime mapping to avoid unsafe routes, and our AutoAlert to feel more secure when commuting late at night. Our AI route tracking also learns her preferred bus routes and suggests Katie with faster and more familiar routes.

Paul

Age: 45

Occupation: Office Worker

Location: Cork, Ireland

Paul drives 40 minutes to and from work every day and is often stuck in traffic making him late. This frustrates him and he wishes for an easier way to avoid heavy traffic. He is concerned about the rising petrol prices and wants to be able to find affordable options near him.

How NavRide Helps: NavRides AI traffic prediction suggests quicker and less congested routes based on live traffic updates, which will help Paul make his early morning meeting on time. Paul can also use NavRides petrol price tracker to help him find the cheapest petrol stations around him. Our smart navigation will help Paul plan a faster and cost-efficient daily commute

Maria

Age: 28

Occupation: Tourist

Location: Spain (Visiting Ireland)

Maria is excited to be visiting Ireland for the first time, but she is unfamiliar with the public transport systems and wants an easy way to visit different parts of the country. She also finds it difficult to understand the Leap Card top-up system. She is also concerned for her safety as she is travelling alone.

How NavRide Helps: NavRide can help Maria to have less stress as she explores Ireland as we have an easy way to find public transport routes, along with Leap Card integration so she can easily check her balance and top it up. NavRide’s crime mapping will provide Maria with safe routes by suggesting well-lit and safer areas as she travels at night

Our Mission

At NavRide, we're transforming daily travel through AI-powered navigation that prioritizes safety, efficiency, and cost-saving. Born from the frustration of everyday commuters, our app combines cutting-edge technology with deep local insights to help 3.2 million Irish commuters navigate smarter and safer.

AI Smart Mapping

Experience intelligent route planning with AI-driven maps that adapt to your needs.

Route Safety Feature

Stay safe with real-time hazard alerts and safety-focused routes.

Innovation at NavRide

NavRide is revolutionizing the way people travel by combining cutting-edge AI technology with user-centric design. Our platform offers intelligent route planning, real-time hazard alerts, and cost-saving features like petrol pricing tracking and Leap Card top-ups. With a focus on safety, efficiency, and sustainability, NavRide is committed to making daily commutes smarter, safer, and more convenient for millions of users.
